Terms & Conditions

Last updated: March 2026

1. Bookings and Quotes

All cleaning services are provided by Jolly Bubble & Shine based in Norwich, Norfolk. When you request a quote, we will aim to provide an estimate based on the information you provide about your home and cleaning needs. Quotes are generally valid for 14 days from the date issued. A quote is not a binding agreement until you have confirmed a booking with us.

2. Pricing and Price Variations

Our published prices are a guide only. Final pricing may vary based on:

  • The actual size and condition of your property
  • The level of cleaning required
  • Any heavy build-up (mould, limescale, pet hair, etc.)
  • Number of rooms and bathrooms
  • Travel distance from Norwich

We will confirm the final price before starting any work. If we discover the property requires significantly more work than estimated, we will discuss this with you before proceeding.

3. Access to Your Property

You agree to provide us with safe and reasonable access to your property at the agreed time. You are responsible for disarming any alarms, providing keys or access codes, and ensuring the property is safe for us to work in. If access cannot be reasonably arranged, we may need to reschedule or charge a call-out fee.

4. Cancellations and Notice Periods

If you need to cancel a confirmed booking, please provide at least 48 hours' notice. Cancellations with less than 48 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee equivalent to 50% of the quoted price. Bookings cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged in full.

We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule if we are unable to work due to circumstances beyond our control, and will reschedule at the earliest opportunity.

5. Payment Terms

Payment is due on completion of the service unless other arrangements have been agreed. We accept cash, bank transfer, and contactless payment. A valid contact number and address must be provided before any work begins.

6. Products and Equipment

We bring our own professional cleaning products and equipment. If you prefer us to use specific products or have allergies or sensitivities, please inform us during the quote stage. We will do our best to accommodate reasonable requests. You may also request that we use only your own products, though this may affect the quality and time required for the clean.

7. Satisfaction and Quality

We aim to deliver high-quality cleaning every time. If you are not satisfied with any aspect of our work, please contact us within 24 hours of completion. We will discuss the issue with you and will return to remedy any legitimate concerns at no extra cost. We cannot be held responsible for issues reported more than 24 hours after completion.

8. Damage and Liability

We carry appropriate insurance and take care to protect your property. However, we cannot be held responsible for:

  • Existing damage to your property that was not reasonably visible before we started
  • Damage to fragile or valuable items that were not disclosed or made safe
  • Damage caused by your own belongings, pets, or property defects
  • Items that go missing during cleaning (we recommend securing valuable items)

If we cause accidental damage during cleaning, please report it immediately and we will work with our insurance to resolve the issue fairly. Our liability is limited to the value of the cleaning service provided.

9. Right to Refuse Work

We reserve the right to refuse or discontinue work if:

  • The property presents a health or safety risk
  • Access to the property is unsafe or obstructed
  • Behaviour towards us is threatening or abusive
  • The scope of work is significantly different from what was quoted for

In such cases, we will discuss this with you before deciding whether to proceed.
