Cookies Policy

Last updated: March 2026

How We Use Cookies

We want your experience with Jolly Bubble & Shine to be smooth and secure. Like most websites, we use a few small files called cookies to help our site work properly for you. These cookies are not used for advertising or tracking you around the web.

What Cookies Do We Use?

We only use essential cookies on this website. These help with things like:

  • Remembering what you type into forms (so you don’t lose your message if you refresh)
  • Keeping the site secure and running smoothly
  • Saving your accessibility or display preferences (if you set them)

We do not use cookies for advertising, analytics, or to build a profile about you.

Third-Party Cookies

We don’t use Google Analytics or any advertising cookies. If you click a link to another website (like Facebook or Instagram), those sites may set their own cookies. We can’t control what other sites do, so please check their policies if you’re curious.

How to Control Cookies

If you’d rather not use cookies, you can change your browser settings to block or delete them. Just be aware that some parts of our website might not work as expected if you turn off essential cookies. For help, see:

  • Google Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Your Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. We don’t use cookies to track you, and we never sell your information. If you have any questions or worries, just get in touch.

Updates

If we ever change how we use cookies, we’ll update this page and let you know if it’s important.
