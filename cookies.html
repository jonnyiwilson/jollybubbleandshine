Cookies Policy

Last updated: March 2026

How We Use Cookies

We want your experience with Jolly Bubble & Shine to be smooth and secure. At the moment, this website is designed to work without non-essential cookies. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking you around the web.

What Cookies Do We Use?

At present, we do not intentionally set analytics or advertising cookies on this website. If our hosting provider or form provider uses strictly necessary technical cookies, they are used only to keep the site secure and functioning properly.

We do not use cookies for advertising, analytics, or to build a profile about you.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use Google Analytics or any advertising cookies. If you click a link to another website, such as Facebook or Instagram, those sites may set their own cookies. We cannot control what other sites do, so please check their policies if you want more detail.

How to Control Cookies

If you would rather not use cookies, you can change your browser settings to block or delete them. Just be aware that some parts of websites in general may not work as expected if essential cookies are turned off. For help, see:

  • Google Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Your Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. We do not use cookies to track you, and we never sell your information. If you have any questions or concerns, please get in touch.

Updates

If we ever change how we use cookies, we will update this page and let you know if the change is significant.
